R5T.Bristol - An experimental console-app and accompanying library for capturing ISO-6346 information.

ISO-6346:1995 is the standard for shipping container identification markings. Information can be found publically here:

	* Wikipedia: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_6346
	* ISO.org (paywall): https://www.iso.org/standard/20453.html

Goals

The Bristol project and library is meant to capture information from the standard in data and code, and to supply a library of functionality for working with the standard, including:

	* Check-digit calculation and verification.
